About Us

Welcome to Bengaluru City University's Resume Builder website, designed and developed as part of a final year project by Sugandha, a Master's student in Computer Science.

Our mission is to empower individuals with tools to create compelling resumes that stand out in today's competitive job market. Whether you're a student, a job seeker, or a seasoned professional, our platform offers customizable resume templates, ATS screening tools, and skills assessment services to help you achieve your career goals.

With a commitment to quality and user satisfaction, we provide responsive customer support and continuously update our features to align with industry standards.
